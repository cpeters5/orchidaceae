Guideline for Photo Submission
  • Must be correctly identified
  • Public photos are chosen based on the quality of the photo. What considered good photography:
    • In focus, good Lighting and brightnerss and natural color
    • Easch photo should showcase the unique character of the orchid. E.g. flower, florescences, closeup of parts, leaf, plant or habitat.
    • The photo should be void of added text such as orchid name or date. If you must add copyright credit, it should be in small font placed in the lower right corner.
    • Plain Simple background, preferable solid dark color.
    Photos following these guidelines will be chosen for public display over those that are not.
